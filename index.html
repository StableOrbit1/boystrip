BoysTrip is the ultimate trip companion for a group who, let's be honest, likely can't organize a two car parade and forgot to take out money before the trip.

Tell your crew when they need to be, where they need to be, and how much they owe you when you work them over on the links. All the details, and few more surprises, packed into one customized trip app.

Quick Access

🍎 Apple iOS

  1. Tap the Share icon at the bottom of Safari.
  2. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen .
  3. Tap Add in the top right corner.

🤖 Android

  1. Tap the Menu icon (⋮) at the top right of Chrome.
  2. Tap Add to Home screen or Install App.
  3. Follow the prompts to add it to your launcher.
